Privacy Policy for RideReady: Smart Bike Weather

Last Updated: May 26, 2026

At Mungalabs, we respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy applies specifically to the RideReady: Smart Bike Weather application, developed and published under the Mungalabs developer account.

Information We Collect

  • Device and usage data: Such as app version, crash reports, and general analytics. We use Google Firebase (Analytics and Crashlytics) to collect non-identifiable technical data to improve app performance.
  • Location data: Used to suggest nearby cycling routes, provide weather conditions, and calculate ride distances.
  • Weekly Progress Data: Local storage of your daily cycling achievements to visualize your weekly activity streak.
  • Advertising data: Ads are provided by Google AdMob and comply with family-friendly standards.
  • Advertising ID: RideReady: Smart Bike Weather uses the Google Mobile Ads SDK, which may access the device’s Advertising ID to display personalized or non-personalized ads. The Advertising ID is not linked to personally identifiable information.

Ride Tracking & Background Location

RideReady includes a tracking feature to calculate cycling distance and Speed. This requires Background Location access to function when your screen is off. We use this to provide real-time feedback and display Breadcrumb trails on the Map.

Breadcrumb Trail Storage

The Breadcrumb trail of your most recent ride is stored locally on your device. This allows you to view your previous travels on the map. This data is never sent to our servers and is cleared when you start a new ride.

Floating Bubble & Display Over Other Apps

The Floating Bubble allows you to view your speed while using other apps. This requires "Display over other apps" permission. No data is collected or transmitted through this overlay; it is purely a visual tool.

Social Sharing

The Share feature allows you to manually export ride statistics. This action is entirely user-initiated. RideReady does not collect or have access to the content shared.

User Data Control

Users have full control over tracking data. You can permanently clear your previous ride and total distance at any time using the Reset button within the app.

Notifications & Alerts

We may send you notifications regarding cycling weather and tracking status. We use your device's location to determine relevance. You can opt-out at any time in device settings.

Third-Party Services

RideReady integrates external services:

  • Open-Meteo API: Used for weather forecasts. Coordinates may be processed to deliver accurate data.
  • Google Firebase: Used for crash reporting and app usage analytics.

Children’s Privacy

RideReady is not specifically designed for children. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. Any data collected is limited to essential app functionality.

How We Use Information

  • To improve app performance and user experience.
  • To provide location-based cycling features and weather forecasts.
  • To display relevant ads.
  • We do not sell or share personal information with third parties.

Data Security

  • We take reasonable steps to protect your information.
  • No personally identifiable information is stored directly by RideReady.
