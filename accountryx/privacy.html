Privacy Policy for Accountryx

At Mungalabs, we believe that education should be accessible and private. This Privacy Policy applies to the Accountryx application, an educational platform designed to help users organize and track their learning journey through curated video content.

1. Information We Collect

  • Learning Progress: We store data regarding the videos you have watched, your Saved Videos, the time you have spent learning, your Quiz Results, and your Study Notes. This data is stored 100% locally on your device using Android DataStore and is not transmitted to our servers.
  • YouTube Interaction: Accountryx uses YouTube API Services to fetch educational content. When you search or view videos, non-personally identifiable information is processed to deliver the content to you.
  • Lesson Sharing: When you use the "Share" feature, the app generates a standard Android sharing intent. No data regarding your contacts or shared content is collected or stored by Mungalabs.
  • Device and Usage Data: We use Firebase Crashlytics and Analytics to collect basic analytics such as app version and anonymized crash reports to improve the stability and performance of the application.
  • Advertising ID: Accountryx uses the Google Mobile Ads SDK (AdMob). This SDK may access your device’s Advertising ID to show relevant ads. This ID is not linked to your name or email and can be reset in your Android device settings.

2. YouTube API Services

Our app uses YouTube API Services to provide video content. By using Accountryx, you agree to be bound by the YouTube Terms of Service and the Google Privacy Policy.

We do not access, collect, or store any private information from your YouTube account. Users can revoke the App’s access to their data via the Google security settings page at https://security.google.com/settings/security/permissions.

3. Local Data Storage & User Control

Your privacy is our priority. Accountryx is designed so that your Learning History, Progress Stats, and Personalized Notes remain on your own device. We do not have access to your specific learning habits or history.

Data Control: You can clear your learning progress at any time by clearing the application cache/data in your Android system settings or by deleting the app.

4. Advertising & Third-Party Services

Accountryx integrates the following third-party services to support the development of this free app:

  • Google AdMob: Used to display advertisements appropriate for a general educational audience.
  • Firebase: Used for real-time app health monitoring and performance improvements.
  • YouTube Player SDK: Used to provide the integrated video playback experience within the app.

5. Children’s Privacy

Accountryx is an educational tool suitable for learners of various ages.

  • We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.
  • All learning data is stored locally on the device, ensuring the highest level of privacy for younger users.
  • Advertisements are configured to follow "Family-Friendly" and educational guidelines.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect any data processed by the app. Since Accountryx does not require a user account or cloud login, your personal identity remains anonymous to us.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our app features or legal requirements. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
