Privacy Policy for Accountryx

At Mungalabs, we believe that education should be accessible and private. This Privacy Policy applies to the Accountryx application, an educational platform designed to help users organize and track their learning journey through curated video content.

1. Information We Collect

  • Learning Progress: We store data regarding the videos you have watched, the time you have spent learning, and your quiz results. This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.
  • YouTube Interaction: Accountryx uses the YouTube Data API to fetch educational content. When you search or view videos, non-personally identifiable information is processed to deliver the content to you.
  • Device and Usage Data: We may collect basic analytics such as app version and crash reports to improve the stability of the application.
  • Advertising ID: Accountryx uses the Google Mobile Ads SDK (AdMob). This SDK may access your device’s Advertising ID to show relevant ads. This ID is not linked to your name or email and can be reset in your Android device settings.

2. YouTube API Services

Our app uses YouTube API Services to provide video content. By using Accountryx, you agree to be bound by the YouTube Terms of Service and the Google Privacy Policy.

We do not access, collect, or store any private information from your YouTube account. We only access public content (playlists and videos) available via the official API to facilitate your learning.

3. Local Data Storage & User Control

Your privacy is our priority. Accountryx is designed so that your Learning History and Progress Stats remain on your own device. We do not have access to your specific learning habits or history.

Data Control: You can clear your learning progress at any time by clearing the application cache/data in your Android system settings or by using the reset options within the app (where available).

4. Advertising & Third-Party Services

Accountryx integrates the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: Used to display advertisements that support the development of this free app. Ads are filtered to ensure they are appropriate for a general educational audience.
  • YouTube Player SDK: Used to provide the video playback experience within the app.

5. Children’s Privacy

Accountryx is an educational tool suitable for learners of various ages.

  • We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.
  • All learning data is stored locally on the device, ensuring the highest level of privacy for younger users.
  • Advertisements are configured to follow "Family-Friendly" guidelines.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect any data processed by the app. Since Accountryx does not require a user account or cloud login, your personal identity remains anonymous to us.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our app features or legal requirements. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
