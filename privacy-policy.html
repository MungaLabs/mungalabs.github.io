Privacy Policy for RideReady: Smart Bike Weather

At Mungalabs, we respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy applies to the RideReady application.

Information We Collect

  • Location data: Used to suggest cycling routes, provide weather, and calculate ride distances.
  • Advertising data: Provided by Google AdMob.

Ride Tracking & Background Location

RideReady includes a feature to calculate cycling distance. This requires Background Location access to function when your screen is off. We do not store or share this location history; it is kept only on your device.

User Data Control & Deletion

Users have full control over their tracking data. You can permanently clear your previous ride and total accumulated distance at any time using the Reset button within the app tracking panel.
