Privacy Policy for RideReady: Smart Bike Weather

At Mungalabs, we respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy applies specifically to the RideReady: Smart Bike Weather application, developed and published under the Mungalabs developer account.

Information We Collect

  • Device and usage data: Such as app version, crash reports, and general analytics.
  • Location data: Used to suggest nearby cycling routes, provide weather conditions, and calculate ride distances.
  • Advertising data: Ads are provided by Google AdMob and comply with family-friendly standards.
  • Advertising ID: RideReady: Smart Bike Weather uses the Google Mobile Ads SDK, which may access the device’s Advertising ID to display personalized or non-personalized ads. The Advertising ID is not linked to personally identifiable information and is only used for advertising and analytics purposes. Users can reset or disable the Advertising ID in their device settings.

Ride Tracking & Background Location

RideReady includes a tracking feature to calculate cycling distance. This requires Background Location access to function correctly when your screen is off or when the app is in the background. We do not collect, store on our servers, or share this location history; it is processed and kept only locally on your device.

User Data Control

Users have full control over their tracking data. You can permanently clear your previous ride and total accumulated distance at any time using the Reset button within the tracking panel of the app.

Notifications & Alerts

RideReady may send you notifications regarding cycling weather conditions, ride recommendations, and active tracking status. We use your device's location to determine when these alerts are relevant to you. You can opt-out of these notifications at any time through your device settings.

Third-Party Services

RideReady: Smart Bike Weather integrates external services to provide features:

  • Open-Meteo API: Used to retrieve weather information. Open-Meteo may process non-identifiable data such as location coordinates to deliver accurate forecasts.

We do not control how third-party services handle data, but we only integrate providers that comply with privacy and security standards.

Children’s Privacy

RideReady: Smart Bike Weather is not specifically designed for children, but if accessed by younger users:

  • We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.
  • Any data collected is limited to what is necessary for app functionality (such as non-identifiable usage statistics).
  • Ads shown are reviewed to ensure they are appropriate for a general audience.

How We Use Information

  • To improve app performance and user experience.
  • To provide location-based cycling route suggestions, weather features, and ride distance tracking.
  • To display relevant ads (if applicable).
  • We do not sell or share personal information with third parties.

Data Security

  • We take reasonable steps to protect your information.
  • No personally identifiable information is stored directly by RideReady: Smart Bike Weather.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.
