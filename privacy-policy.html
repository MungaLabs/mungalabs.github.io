Privacy Policy for RideReady: Smart Bike Weather

Last updated: April 28, 2026

At Mungalabs, we respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy applies specifically to the RideReady: Smart Bike Weather application, developed and published under the Mungalabs developer account.

1. Information We Collect

  • Device and usage data: Such as app version, crash reports, and general analytics.
  • Location data: Used to provide local weather conditions, smart ride recommendations, and calculate ride distances.
  • Weekly Progress Data: Local storage of your daily cycling achievements to visualize your weekly activity streak.
  • Advertising data: Ads are provided by Google AdMob and comply with family-friendly standards.
  • Advertising ID: This app uses the Google Mobile Ads SDK, which may access the device’s Advertising ID to display personalized or non-personalized ads. The Advertising ID is not linked to personally identifiable information and is only used for advertising and analytics purposes.

2. Ride Tracking & Background Location

RideReady: Smart Bike Weather includes a tracking feature to calculate cycling distance and speed. This requires Background Location access to function correctly when your screen is off or when the app is in the background. We use this data to provide real-time pace feedback via Motion Cues and to display Breadcrumb trails on the Map.

Local Processing: We do not collect, store on our servers, or share this location history; it is processed and kept only locally on your device.

3. Floating Bubble & Overlay

To keep you informed during your ride, the Floating Bubble feature allows you to view your speed and status while using other applications. This requires the "Display over other apps" permission. No data is collected, stored, or transmitted through this overlay; it is purely a visual tool for your convenience.

4. Social Sharing

The Share feature allows you to manually export your ride statistics to third-party applications. This action is entirely user-initiated. RideReady does not collect or have access to the content shared through this feature.

5. User Data Control

Users have full control over their tracking data. You can permanently clear your previous ride and total accumulated distance at any time using the Reset button within the tracking panel of the app.

6. Notifications & Alerts

The app may send you notifications regarding cycling weather conditions, ride recommendations, and active tracking status. We use your device's location to determine when these alerts are relevant to you. You can opt-out at any time through device settings.

7. Third-Party Services

We integrate the following services to provide app features:

  • Google Play Services & Google Maps
  • Google AdMob
  • Open-Meteo Weather API

8. Children’s Privacy

RideReady is not specifically designed for children. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. Any data collected is limited to what is necessary for app functionality, and ads are reviewed for a general audience.

9. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information. No personally identifiable information is stored directly by RideReady: Smart Bike Weather on external servers.
