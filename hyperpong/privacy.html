Privacy Policy for Hyper Pong Speed Challenge

At Mungalabs, we believe that gaming should be accessible and private. This Privacy Policy applies to the Hyper Pong Speed Challenge application, a fast-paced arcade game designed to test your reflexes and speed.

1. Local Data Management

The core of your gaming experience is managed locally on your device via Android SharedPreferences. We utilize this to store:

  • High Scores: Your best performance metrics and speed records.
  • Virtual Currency (Gold): Balances earned through active gameplay.
  • Shop Progress: Information regarding unlocked skins and power-up levels.
  • Milestones: Your Prestige Ranks and 7-Day Streak achievements.

This data is stored 100% locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. It never leaves your device unless you manually clear your application cache or delete the app.

2. Professional Integration & Analytics

To provide a high-quality, stable experience, we integrate industry-standard third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: Used to display advertisements. AdMob may access your device’s Advertising ID to provide relevant content. You can manage these preferences in your Android device settings.
  • Firebase Analytics: Used to understand general user engagement, such as identifying popular game zones to adjust difficulty for a better experience. No personal identifiers are used.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Used to maintain our "Bulletproof" stability standard. This service sends anonymized crash reports to help us fix bugs instantly.

3. Children’s Privacy & Safety

Hyper Pong Speed Challenge is designed for a general audience and strictly complies with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the Google Play Families Policy.

  • We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.
  • All game progress and achievements are stored locally on the device, ensuring the highest level of privacy.
  • Advertisements are configured to follow standard family-friendly guidelines.

4. Data Security

We take Data Safety seriously. Although we do not require a user account or cloud login, all communication between the app and third-party services (Firebase/AdMob) is encrypted in transit using secure HTTPS/TLS protocols.

5. User Control

Your privacy is our priority. You have full control over your data. You can clear your game progress, high scores, and currency at any time by clearing the application data in your Android system settings or by deleting the app.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in game features or legal requirements. Updates will be posted on this page.
