Privacy Policy

Munga Labs operates the Hyper Pong Speed Challenge mobile application. This Service is provided at no cost and is intended for use as is. This document outlines our commitment to your privacy and details how data is handled within our ecosystem.

Note: We prioritize your privacy. We do not collect personally identifiable information directly. Your game data belongs to you and is stored securely on your local device.

1. Local Data Management

The core of your gaming experience is managed locally on your device via Android SharedPreferences. We utilize this to store:

  • High Scores and performance metrics.
  • Virtual Currency (Gold) balances earned through gameplay.
  • Shop Progress, including unlocked skins and power-up levels.
  • Prestige Ranks and 7-Day Streak milestones.

This data never leaves your device unless you manually clear your app storage.

2. Professional Integration & Analytics

To provide a high-quality experience, we integrate industry-standard third-party services:

A. Google AdMob

We utilize Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may use your Advertising ID to provide relevant ad content to improve your experience.

B. Firebase Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to understand user engagement and optimize game difficulty for our Top 1% Speed Elite.

C. Firebase Crashlytics

To maintain our "Bulletproof" stability standard, we use Crashlytics for anonymized crash reporting and instant bug resolution.

3. Children’s Privacy & Safety

Our Service strictly complies with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the Google Play Families Policy. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

4. Data Security

All communication with third-party services is encrypted in transit using secure HTTPS/TLS protocols to ensure your data remains protected.
